The interior art style — mascot + diagrams

Same friendly snake, three treatments. Whatever you pick governs the recurring mascot ("Sparky" the guide), the full-page chapter-opener illustrations, and every technical diagram in the book. Each card previews the mascot plus a tiny 3-node diagram so you can judge the figures too. Backgrounds are light to match the Daylight interior.

Style 1

Friendly Flat

Clean solid shapes, rounded forms, soft shadows. Cheerful and crisp — the safest match for the Daylight cover. Diagrams: filled boxes with green header bars. My recommendation.

Style 2

Luminous Pop

Flat shapes plus soft gradients, a gentle glow, and amber firefly sparks — brings a touch of the "Aurora" magic into the bright book. A bit more premium and energetic.

Style 3

Editorial Line + Spot

Lighter line-art with a single green spot-color and lots of whitespace. Minimal, sophisticated, very modern. Diagrams read like a refined reference manual.
