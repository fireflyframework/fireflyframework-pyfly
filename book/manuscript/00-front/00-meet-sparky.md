## Meet Sparky

Sparky is the friendly snake mascot of the Firefly Framework — a small, bright-eyed serpent who has wound his way through more enterprise Python codebases than he cares to count. He pops up at chapter openers to set the scene and appears in callouts whenever a concept deserves a moment of extra care. Sparky does not lecture; he points, grins, and gets out of your way. Think of him as the colleague who has already hit the sharp edges so you do not have to.
